Packaged Ramen

Description

Ramen noodles are a packaged, instant type of noodle made from wheat flour, various vegetable oils and flavorings. The noodles are pre-cooked, meaning they have been steamed and then air dried or fried to shorten cooking time for consumers.

Ingredients

  • 1 ramen pack (with veggie and seasoning packets)
  • 11⁄2 cups of water
  • scallions (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring water in a pot to a boil.
  2. Pour out all ramen contents into the pot.
  3. Cook noodles for ~8-10 minutes or until done
  4. Garnish with scallions and enjoy!
